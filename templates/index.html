Google Sheets ⇄ Database

The Google Sheet is the canonical store; the local DuckDB is the working copy. These buttons sync between them.

Upload · DB → Sheets

Push every table in the local DB back to its matching worksheet. Overwrites everything in those worksheets.

Download · Sheets → DB

Pull every worksheet from the Google Sheet into the local DB. Overwrites the local DB. Use after editing the Sheet by hand or when a teammate uploaded changes.
