Google Sheets ⇄ Database

The Google Sheet is the canonical store; the local DuckDB is the working copy. These buttons sync between them.

Upload · DB → Sheets

Push every table in the local DB back to its matching worksheet. Overwrites everything in those worksheets.

⚠ Download · Sheets → DB

Not for everyday use. Pulls every worksheet from the Google Sheet into the local DB, overwriting everything the server has accumulated since the last upload — including new students added through this UI, attendance marks, push-absent re-registrations, and purchase history. Reserved for the rare case where you've manually edited the Sheet and want the server to match.

Day-to-day, all your changes live in this server's local DB and you should use Upload to push them to the Sheet, not the other way round.

Payment is recorded in the purchase history. Setting Classes to N books the student into this class plus the next N-1 occurrences, and the full N counts towards their paid total (so unmatched credits show as “unassigned” on the Student tab).
